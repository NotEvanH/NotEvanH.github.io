Mr Mcardle Memorial

Our year in Edmund East wouldn’t have been the same without our amazing tutor teacher, whose tall height and energetic personality always made him easy to spot, both in the classroom and out on the field. His love for rugby and gridiron brought a unique passion to our lessons, especially in science, where his dedication and skill as a teacher were clear in every experiment and explanation. He made science come alive for us! And, of course, we’ll always remember his signature greeting, ‘What’s up, pham?’ that instantly made everyone feel like part of a phamily. Thank you for all the laughs, guidance, and unforgettable memories this year!
